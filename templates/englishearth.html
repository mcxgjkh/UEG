Introduction to the "Wandering Earth Project"

Friends from all over the world:

Welcome to the official website of the "Wandering Earth Project"


This is the official website of the "Wandering Earth Project" jointly created by the former United Nations permanent five and 23 other countries around the world

Due to the unprecedented high temperature of the sun, we hope to allow the Earth to escape the solar system, so we have launched the 'Monthly Plan'

Establish three lunar engines at the equator of the lunar surface to drive the moon away from Earth and protect it from the effects of tides when escaping

Next, the "Mountain Moving Plan" was launched to build 12000 planetary engines on Earth, driving the Earth away from the solar system

Our goal is to travel 4.2 light-years away to Centaurus α Star Cb (neighboring star b), in 2500 years, our descendants will arrive at a new home

During this period, there will be 100 generations living on the road, they will work very hard, but this is valuable. May the glory of humanity endure forever

Thank you to the Earth coalition government for bringing us together, and thank you to the solar crisis for helping each other move forward
